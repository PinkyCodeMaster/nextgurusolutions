## Networking Best Practices

We adhere to networking best practices to ensure the reliability, security, and efficiency of your network:

1. **Layered Security**: We implement multiple layers of security to protect against various threats.
2. **Regular Updates**: We keep all network devices and software up-to-date to patch vulnerabilities.
3. **Network Segmentation**: We separate different parts of your network to contain potential security breaches.
4. **Redundancy**: We design networks with redundancy to ensure business continuity in case of failures.
5. **Performance Monitoring**: We continuously monitor network performance to identify and address issues proactively.
6. **Documentation**: We maintain detailed network documentation for easier troubleshooting and future upgrades.
