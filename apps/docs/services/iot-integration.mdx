## IoT Technologies We Work With

At Net Guru Solutions, we leverage a range of IoT technologies to create robust, scalable solutions:

- **Hardware**: Arduino, Raspberry Pi, industrial-grade sensors and actuators
- **Connectivity**: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, LoRaWAN, Cellular (4G/5G), Zigbee
- **Cloud Platforms**: AWS IoT, Google Cloud IoT, Microsoft Azure IoT
- **Data Analytics**: TensorFlow, Apache Spark, Tableau
- **Security**: Encryption, secure boot, device authentication

## IoT Integration Best Practices

We adhere to IoT best practices to ensure the security, reliability, and scalability of your IoT solution:

1. **Security-First Approach**: Implement robust security measures at every layer of the IoT stack.
2. **Scalable Architecture**: Design solutions that can easily grow with your business needs.
3. **Data Privacy Compliance**: Ensure all data collection and processing complies with relevant regulations (e.g., GDPR).
4. **Edge Computing**: Utilize edge computing where appropriate to reduce latency and bandwidth usage.
5. **Interoperability**: Design systems that can easily integrate with other technologies and platforms.
6. **Energy Efficiency**: Optimize power consumption, especially for battery-operated devices.
