## Email Marketing Tools We Use

- **Klaviyo**: Perfect for e-commerce businesses, with powerful segmentation and integration capabilities.

We'll help you choose the right tool based on your specific needs and integrate it seamlessly with your existing systems.

## Email Marketing Best Practices

We adhere to email marketing best practices to ensure the success of your campaigns:

1. **Permission-based marketing**: We only send emails to subscribers who have explicitly opted in to receive them.
2. **Mobile optimization**: All our email templates are responsive and look great on any device.
3. **Clear call-to-actions**: We design emails with clear, compelling CTAs to drive desired actions.
4. **A/B testing**: We regularly test different elements of your emails to optimize performance.
5. **Personalization**: We use subscriber data to create personalized, relevant content.
6. **Compliance**: All our email marketing practices comply with GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and other relevant regulations.
