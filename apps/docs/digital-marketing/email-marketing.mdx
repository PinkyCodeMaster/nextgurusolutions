## Email Marketing Best Practices

1. **Personalization**: Use subscriber data to personalize email content and subject lines.
2. **Mobile Optimization**: Ensure all emails are responsive and look great on mobile devices.
3. **Segmentation**: Divide your email list into segments for more targeted messaging.
4. **Value-First Approach**: Prioritize providing value to your subscribers over constant promotion.
5. **Clear Call-to-Actions**: Use clear, compelling CTAs to guide subscribers towards desired actions.
6. **Consistent Branding**: Maintain consistent branding across all your email communications.
7. **List Hygiene**: Regularly clean your email list to maintain high deliverability rates.
8. **Compliance**: Adhere to email marketing regulations like GDPR and CAN-SPAM.
