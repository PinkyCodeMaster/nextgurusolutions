# Setting Up a New Account

Welcome to Net Guru Solutions! Follow this guide to set up your new account and start leveraging our digital services for your business growth.

## Account Creation Process

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="1. Sign Up">
    - Visit [https://portal.netgurusolutions.com/signup](https://portal.netgurusolutions.com/signup)
    - Click on "Create New Account"
    - Enter your business email, full name, and choose a secure password
    - Accept the terms of service and privacy policy
    - Click "Create Account"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="2. Email Verification">
    - Check your email inbox for a verification message from Net Guru Solutions
    - Click on the verification link in the email
    - If you don't see the email, check your spam folder
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="building" title="3. Business Information">
    After verification, you'll be prompted to enter:
    - Company name
    - Business type
    - Number of employees
    - Industry
    - Primary business goals
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="sliders" title="4. Service Selection">
    - Choose the services you're interested in (Web Design, Email Marketing, IT Support, etc.)
    - Select your preferred support plan
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="credit-card" title="5. Billing Information">
    - Enter your billing details
    - Choose your payment method (credit card, PayPal, or invoice for enterprise clients)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="check-double" title="6. Account Review">
    - Review all entered information
    - Confirm your selections
    - Submit your account setup
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
