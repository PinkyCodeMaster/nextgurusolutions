# Setting Up Your Business Emails

Professional email addresses are crucial for your business credibility. Learn how to set up and manage your business email accounts with Net Guru Solutions.

## Email Setup Process

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="globe" title="1. Choose Your Domain">
    - If you already have a domain, we'll use that for your email addresses
    - If you need a new domain, we can help you purchase and register one
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="2. Select Email Platform">
    Choose from our supported email platforms:
    - Google Workspace (formerly G Suite)
    - Microsoft 365
    - Our own Net Guru Mail solution
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="3. Create Email Accounts">
    - Decide on email addresses (e.g., firstname@yourdomain.com, info@yourdomain.com)
    - Specify the number of accounts needed
    - Set initial passwords for each account
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="gears" title="4. Configure DNS Settings">
    We'll handle the technical setup:
    - Updating MX records
    - Setting up SPF, DKIM, and DMARC for improved deliverability and security
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="mobile" title="5. Set Up Devices">
    We'll guide you through setting up your email on various devices:
    - Desktop email clients (Outlook, Apple Mail, etc.)
    - Mobile devices (iOS and Android)
    - Web browsers for webmail access
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting Common Email Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="circle-exclamation" title="Unable to Send/Receive Emails">
    - Check your internet connection
    - Verify your email settings (SMTP, IMAP/POP3)
    - Ensure your password is correct
    - Contact our support if issues persist
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="circle-exclamation" title="Emails Marked as Spam">
    - Check if your domain has proper SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records
    - Avoid using spam trigger words in your emails
    - Maintain a good sender reputation
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="circle-exclamation" title="Storage Limit Reached">
    - Archive or delete old emails
    - Empty your trash and spam folders
    - Consider upgrading your email storage plan
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
