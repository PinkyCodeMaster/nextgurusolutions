# Logging Into Your Account

Accessing your Net Guru Solutions account is quick and secure. Follow these steps to log in and start managing your digital services.

## Step-by-Step Login Process

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="globe" title="1. Visit our login page">
    Open your web browser and navigate to [https://portal.netgurusolutions.com/login](https://portal.netgurusolutions.com/login)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="user" title="2. Enter your credentials">
    - In the username field, enter the email address associated with your account
    - In the password field, enter your secure password
    - Ensure "Remember me" is checked if you're on a personal, secure device
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="key" title="3. Two-Factor Authentication (if enabled)">
    If you've set up two-factor authentication:
    - You'll be prompted to enter a code
    - Check your authenticator app or SMS for the code
    - Enter the code in the provided field
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="check" title="4. Access your dashboard">
    After successful authentication, you'll be redirected to your personalized dashboard
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Forgot Password" icon="key">
    If you can't remember your password:
    1. Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
    2. Enter your email address
    3. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password
  </Card>
  <Card title="Account Locked" icon="lock">
    If your account is locked due to multiple failed attempts:
    1. Wait 15 minutes before trying again
    2. Use the "Forgot Password" option to reset your credentials
    3. Contact support if issues persist
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Security Best Practices

To keep your account secure:

- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Don't share your login credentials
- Log out when using shared or public computers
- Regularly update your password

Need further assistance? [Contact our support team](/support/contact) for help with account access.
