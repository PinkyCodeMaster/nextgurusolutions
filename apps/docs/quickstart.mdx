## Getting Started with Net Guru Solutions

Learn how to kickstart your digital journey and make the most of our services to boost your online presence.

### Initial Setup

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Create your Net Guru account">
    To begin using our services, you'll need to create an account on our platform. Visit our [client portal](https://portal.netgurusolutions.com) and click on the "Sign Up" button. Fill in your business details and create your login credentials. Once your account is set up, you'll have access to our full range of digital services.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="clipboard-list" title="Complete your business profile">
    After creating your account, it's essential to complete your business profile. This information helps us tailor our services to your specific needs. Navigate to the "Business Profile" section in your dashboard and fill in details such as your industry, target audience, and current digital presence status.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Explore Our Services

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion icon="globe" title="Web Presence">
  Our web presence services include custom website design and development. To get started:
  1. Go to the "Web Services" section in your dashboard.
  2. Click on "Request a Website Quote."
  3. Fill out the brief questionnaire about your website needs.
  4. Our team will review your request and contact you within 24 hours to discuss the next steps.
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="envelope" title="Email Marketing">
  Leverage our email marketing tools to reach and engage your audience effectively:
  1. Navigate to the "Email Marketing" tab in your dashboard.
  2. Click on "Create New Campaign."
  3. Choose from our templates or start from scratch.
  4. Use our intuitive editor to design your email.
  5. Set up your recipient list and schedule your campaign.
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Digital Strategy">
  Get a comprehensive digital strategy tailored to your business:
  1. In your dashboard, go to the "Strategy" section.
  2. Click on "Request Strategy Session."
  3. Choose a convenient time slot for a video call with our digital strategist.
  4. Prepare key information about your business goals and challenges for the session.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Monitor Your Progress

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion icon="chart-bar" title="Access your analytics dashboard">
  Track the performance of your digital initiatives through our analytics dashboard. Here's how:
  1. Log into your Net Guru Solutions account.
  2. Click on the "Analytics" tab in the main navigation.
  3. View key metrics such as website traffic, email open rates, and social media engagement.
  4. Use these insights to refine your digital strategy and improve your online presence.
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="video" title="Schedule a review call">
  Regular check-ins help ensure you're getting the most out of our services:
  1. Go to the "Support" section in your dashboard.
  2. Click on "Schedule Review Call."
  3. Choose a suitable time slot for a video call with your dedicated account manager.
  4. Prepare any questions or concerns you'd like to discuss during the call.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
